About the Poké Collector

The Pokemon Collector is a site where you can view and collect pokemon.

How to Use the Site:

  • You can view your Pokemon roster by clicking "Your Pokemon" in the navigation bar.
  • You can also view more information about the Pokemon by clicking on the Pokemon's card.
  • If you would like to add a Pokemon to your collection, you can do so by clicking on the "Catch Pokemon" button on the Pokemon's page.
  • You can view all the Pokemon in your collection by clicking on the "My Collection" link in the navigation bar.
